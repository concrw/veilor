Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Subscriptions are managed on the web at veilor.kr/pricing. The app itself does not process payments.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Log in to veilor.kr and visit your account settings to manage or cancel your subscription.

I forgot my password.

Use the "Forgot password" link on the login screen, or visit veilor.kr to reset your password.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account inside the app, or email support@veilor.kr with a deletion request.
